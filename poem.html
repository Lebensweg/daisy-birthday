For Daisy

She is like the moon, her beauty shows in the dark,
Her glow, a quiet fire, a beacon through the night.
She moves like the air, swift and unassuming,
Yet her presence lingers, a whisper forever blooming.

She twists and turns like the waves of the sea,
Crashing against the shore, wild and free.
She was never one to shirk a challenge, bold as a lion,
With a heart unyielding, fierce as the sun in July.

She was broken, fixed, and then broken some more,
Yet each crack became a window, a way to love deeper than before.
I've seen her at worse times, yet her beauty is always visible,
A testament to resilience, her spirit indefatigable.

Today, she is a year older, no doubt wiser,
Her laughter a melody, her dreams rising higher.
And though it only gets tougher, I believe in her,
For she is the storm and the calm, a force that will always endure.
